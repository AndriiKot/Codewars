You are a border guard sitting on the 
Canadian border. You were given a list 
of travelers who have arrived at your gate today. 
You know that American, Mexican, and Canadian 
citizens don't need visas, so they can just 
continue their trips. You don't need to check their 
passports for visas! You only need to check 
the passports of citizens of all other countries!

Select names, and countries of origin of all the travelers, excluding anyone from Canada, Mexico, or The US.
